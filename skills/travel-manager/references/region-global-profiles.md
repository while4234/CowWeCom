# Global Region Profiles

Use these profiles to avoid applying one generic international template.

## Hong Kong, Macau, Taiwan

- Hong Kong: MTR, Octopus, Airport Express, cross-border ports, Peak/Disney/Ocean Park reservations, taxis, buses, ferries.
- Macau: Border Gate, Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macau Bridge, Cotai/peninsula routing, hotel shuttles, historic-center walking density, taxi and bus constraints.
- Taiwan: HSR, TRA, MRT, coaches, EasyCard, night markets, island routing, mountain weather, typhoon season.

## Japan

Consider JR, private railways, metro, Shinkansen, IC cards, JR Pass value, reserved seats, last trains, luggage forwarding, cherry blossom, autumn leaves, typhoon/snow season, onsen etiquette, shrine/temple etiquette, restaurant queues, and trash sorting.

## Korea

Consider Seoul/Busan/Jeju differences, subway, KTX, airport rail, T-money, concert/exhibition/fandom routes, cafes and shopping districts, restaurant reservations, and K-ETA/visa/visa-free official verification.

## Southeast Asia

Handle countries separately:

- Thailand: BTS/MRT, Grab, island boats, temple dress, rainy season.
- Singapore: MRT, EZ-Link/SimplyGo, attraction booking, strict local rules, Universal Studios, Marina Bay, Sentosa.
- Malaysia: Kuala Lumpur, Penang, Sabah, Grab, island transport.
- Vietnam: north-south distance, motorbike risk, trains, domestic flights, Ha Long Bay, Da Nang, Ho Chi Minh City, Hanoi.
- Indonesia: Bali traffic, volcanoes, diving, religious holidays, inter-island transport.
- Philippines: island hopping, typhoon season, ferry cancellation risk.

## Europe

Consider Schengen, 90/180 day rule, ETIAS official verification, cross-border trains, low-cost airlines, baggage fees, seat reservation fees, city tax, Sunday closures, museum closure days, strike risk, public-transport ticket inspections, tips, and restaurant hours.

## United States

Consider ESTA/VWP/visa official verification, TSA, entry documents, long city distances, self-driving, parking, rental car insurance, national park timed entry, campsites and hiking permits, tips, sales tax, hotel deposits, credit-card preauthorization, and medical risk.
