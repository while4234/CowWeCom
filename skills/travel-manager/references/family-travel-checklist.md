# Family Travel Checklist

Use this checklist when travelers include children, elderly travelers, pregnancy, wheelchair users, chronic illness, large luggage, or low-intensity preferences.

## Documents And Safety

- [ ] Passport/ID requirements verified without storing full document numbers
- [ ] Visa/entry rules marked as official verification required
- [ ] Travel insurance and emergency contacts
- [ ] Medicine list, prescriptions, allergy notes, and hospital access
- [ ] Destination emergency numbers

## Children

- [ ] Child seats or restraint rules
- [ ] Stroller access and storage
- [ ] Nap windows
- [ ] Shorter walking segments
- [ ] Indoor backup for heat, rain, or poor air quality
- [ ] Child-friendly meals and restrooms

## Elderly Travelers

- [ ] Lower daily density
- [ ] Elevator/escalator availability when possible
- [ ] Taxi or private-transfer fallback
- [ ] Rest windows every 1.5-2.5 hours
- [ ] Avoid late-night arrivals and long transfers
- [ ] Flag altitude, heat, cold, stairs, and long queues

## Pregnancy, Wheelchair, Chronic Illness

- [ ] Step-free route or accessible transfer check
- [ ] Medical facility proximity for remote destinations
- [ ] Low-altitude and low-strain alternatives
- [ ] Weather-sensitive backup
- [ ] Extra buffer for security, boarding, and restroom access

## Luggage And Logistics

- [ ] Large luggage on rail/metro/ferry checked
- [ ] Hotel luggage storage or delivery
- [ ] Station/airport transfer time
- [ ] Laundry and packing light options for long trips
