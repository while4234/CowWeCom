# Mainland China Region Profile

Use this profile for China mainland travel.

## Core Sources

- City routes, POI, ETA, traffic, parking, and route density: `amap-cowwechat`
- China railway and high-speed rail: `plugin-12306-ticket`
- Flights, hotels, attractions, and travel inventory: FlyAI if installed and smoke-tested, otherwise real-time official/OTA pages
- Weather: AMap weather when available; otherwise `quick-weather`

## Planning Rules

- Check route density before finalizing a city itinerary.
- Prefer fewer stops and longer buffers for elderly travelers, children, pregnancy, wheelchair users, chronic illness, high altitude, hot weather, or large luggage.
- Add meal, rest, and restroom windows.
- Flag Spring Festival travel rush, summer holidays, Golden Week, school holidays, concerts, exhibitions, and make-up workdays as risk multipliers.
- For scenic areas, verify real-name reservation, ID/passport entry, ticket inventory, closure days, shuttle rules, and last-entry time.
- For intercity plans, compare rail, flight, car, and bus only when realistic. Do not ignore station-to-hotel transfer time.

## Foreign Visitors In China

When relevant, mention:

- Payment setup: mobile payment, cards, cash backup
- Connectivity: SIM/eSIM, roaming, VPN/legal availability where appropriate
- Hotel registration with passport
- 12306 passport ticketing and station identity checks
- Attraction ID/passport requirements
- Map and translation apps
- Emergency numbers and medical access
